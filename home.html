Welcome to the Highland Hotel

Experience the beauty of the Scottish Highlands

Our Rooms

Our rooms are designed to provide the perfect place to relax and unwind after a day of exploring the Scottish Highlands. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom, a TV, and a coffee maker. Our rooms are also pet-friendly, so you can bring your furry friend along on your adventure.

Our Restaurant

Our restaurant offers a variety of delicious dishes made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Whether you're in the mood for a hearty breakfast, a light lunch, or a gourmet dinner, our restaurant has something for everyone. We also offer a selection of fine wines and craft beers to complement your meal.

Our Spa

Our spa is the perfect place to relax and rejuvenate during your stay. We offer a range of treatments, including massages, facials, and body scrubs, to help you unwind and de-stress. Our spa also features a sauna and a steam room, so you can enjoy a truly indulgent experience.
